Sports-product-sales-analysis



Description

In this project, I concentrated on identifying when and why the highest sales occur. I uncovered some key insights, such as West Gear selling 31% more products than Amazon (which had the lowest sales), driven by a reduced per-unit price. Additionally, men's street footwear outsold women's by 66%. The majority of sales were concentrated in states with the highest number of universities. This Dashboard is made using FP20 Analytics dataset, Tableau, and Figma. To view more dashboards visit my tableau profile.
